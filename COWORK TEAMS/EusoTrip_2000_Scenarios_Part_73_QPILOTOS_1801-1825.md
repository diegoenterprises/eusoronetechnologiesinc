## Scenario IVQ-1801: QPilotOS Module 1 — Admin Command Center

The Command Center synthesizes data from all other 5 QPilotOS modules into a single "flight deck" for executive decision-making. Key capabilities: real-time platform health metrics, anomaly alerting, executive KPI dashboard, and "what-if" scenario modeling.

**Steps:**
1. Super Admin opens QPilotOS Command Center — "flight deck" displays:
   - Active loads in transit: 847 (real-time map with status color coding)
   - Platform health: 99.97% uptime, 3.2ms average API response, 0 critical errors
   - Revenue today: $2.32M (12% above same day last year)
   - Safety: 0 incidents today, 847-day fleet streak for Driver Martinez (longest active)
   - Compliance: 3 driver medical cards expiring this week (flagged for HR action)
2. Anomaly alert: ESANG AI detects unusual pattern — Chicago-area load volume down 23% vs. historical Thursday average. Root cause analysis: major refinery (BP Whiting) undergoing unplanned maintenance → chemical output reduced → fewer outbound loads
3. "What-if" scenario: Super Admin asks — "What happens to Q2 revenue if BP Whiting maintenance extends 30 days?" QPilotOS models: $4.7M revenue impact, 89 loads/month redirected to alternate suppliers, 12 carriers in Chicago area will have excess capacity (recommend: redirect to Michigan lanes)
4. Executive KPI dashboard refreshes every 5 minutes:
   - GMV: $847M YTD ($2.32M today)
   - Net Revenue Retention: 134%
   - Carrier acquisition this month: 47 new (target: 40)
   - Shipper NPS: +67 (target: +50)
   - DOT inspection pass rate: 98.7%
   - The Haul engagement: 87% DAU of registered drivers
5. Board-ready report generator: Super Admin clicks "Generate Board Report" — QPilotOS compiles: financial summary, operational metrics, safety performance, growth trajectory, competitive position, risk factors — formatted for board presentation in <60 seconds
6. Regulatory alert: PHMSA publishes proposed rule change to §172.101 — QPilotOS flags for compliance team review with impact assessment: affects 23% of platform loads, estimated $340K implementation cost
7. Competitive intelligence: Uber Freight pricing detected 5% lower on 3 key lanes — QPilotOS recommends targeted discount for affected shippers to prevent churn
8. Resource allocation: QPilotOS recommends: hire 2 additional enterprise sales reps for Northeast expansion (ROI model: 14x within 12 months based on pipeline data)
9. Cash flow forecast: QPilotOS projects: $4.2M cash position in 30 days (healthy), potential dip to $1.8M in 60 days (2 large shippers on Net-60 terms) — recommends: accelerate collections on 5 overdue accounts ($890K total)
10. End-of-day summary auto-generated and emailed to executive team — key highlights, action items, risk flags

**Expected Outcome:** Complete platform visibility from single dashboard. Anomalies detected and modeled. Board-ready reports in 60 seconds. Proactive risk management.

**Platform Features Tested:** Unified Command Dashboard, Real-Time Platform Health, Anomaly Detection, What-If Scenario Modeling, Executive KPI Dashboard, Board Report Generator, Regulatory Alert System, Competitive Intelligence, Resource Allocation Recommendations, Cash Flow Forecasting

## Scenario IVQ-1802: QPilotOS Module 2 — Compliance Automation
**Company:** Quality Carriers (Tampa, FL) — Full Compliance Suite
**Season:** Continuous | **Time:** Every load, every driver, every vehicle

**Narrative:** QPilotOS Module 2 automates the entire compliance lifecycle for Quality Carriers' 5,400-truck fleet: driver qualification management, vehicle inspection tracking, hazmat training records, shipping paper generation, incident reporting, and audit preparation. Module 2 processes 47,000 compliance events per day across the fleet.

**Steps:**
1. Daily compliance pulse: Module 2 scans all 5,400 DQ files, 8,600 vehicles, and 47,000 daily events
2. Morning alerts dashboard for Quality's Compliance Manager:
   - 23 medical cards expiring within 30 days (drivers auto-scheduled for physicals)
   - 7 vehicles overdue for annual inspection (removed from dispatch queue)
   - 3 drivers with training expiring this week (auto-enrolled in online recurrent training)
   - 1 new PHMSA enforcement notice received (details + recommended response)
3. Real-time load compliance: every load creation triggers 247 compliance checks in <2 seconds
4. Shipping paper automation: 890 shipping papers generated today, all containing: proper shipping name, hazard class, UN number, packing group, quantity, shipper certification, emergency phone — zero manual entry
5. Inspection tracking: 124 roadside inspections this month — Module 2 pulled documentation instantly for inspectors. 98.7% pass rate. 2 violations: both form/manner (ELD annotation issues) — auto-corrected for future
6. Training compliance: §172.704 training for 5,400 drivers + 340 office staff = 5,740 employees. Module 2 tracks: initial training date, recurrent due date (3 years), function-specific completions, security awareness, and in-depth security training. Current compliance: 99.1%
7. Drug/alcohol program: Module 2 manages random selection (50% annual rate), schedules tests, tracks MRO results, monitors Clearinghouse, and manages return-to-duty for 2 drivers currently in SAP program
8. Incident reporting: 3 incidents this month — Module 2 correctly classified: 2 required §171.16 only (written), 1 required §171.15 (telephonic) due to road closure. All reports auto-generated and filed
9. Audit readiness score: Module 2 calculates: 97.4% (up from 89.2% six months ago). Remaining gaps: 14 DQ files missing previous employer verification (auto-requested)
10. Annual compliance cost savings vs. manual: 12,400 hours of compliance staff time eliminated ($930K/year for Quality Carriers)

**Expected Outcome:** 47,000 daily compliance events automated. 99.1% training compliance. 98.7% inspection pass rate. $930K annual savings for single carrier.

**Platform Features Tested:** DQ File Management, Vehicle Inspection Tracking, Training Record Automation, Shipping Paper Generation, Incident Report Automation, Drug/Alcohol Program, Clearinghouse Integration, Audit Readiness Scoring, Compliance Cost Calculator

**ROI Calculation:** Quality Carriers: $930K/year in compliance staff savings + $267K in fine avoidance + $89K in audit preparation savings = $1.29M/year; platform-wide (2,400 carriers): projected $51.2M/year

## Scenarios IVQ-1803 through IVQ-1824: Condensed QPilotOS Module Scenarios

**IVQ-1803: Module 3 — Financial Operations: Automated Invoicing** — QPilotOS generates, sends, and tracks 26,000 invoices per month. Features: auto-generation from load completion data, accessorial charge inclusion, fuel surcharge calculation, volume discount application, shipper-specific GL code mapping, payment terms enforcement, aging tracking, and collections escalation. DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) reduced from 34 days to 21 days.

**IVQ-1804: Module 3 — Financial: Revenue Analytics** — QPilotOS tracks revenue by: lane, shipper, carrier, hazmat class, season, region, load type. Identifies: highest-margin lanes (target for growth), lowest-margin lanes (renegotiate or drop), at-risk accounts (declining volume), expansion opportunities (increasing volume). Monthly revenue analytics report generated for CFO.

**IVQ-1805: Module 3 — Financial: Carrier Settlement Engine** — Processes 1,200 carrier settlements per day through EusoWallet/Stripe Connect. Settlement includes: line haul, fuel surcharge, accessorials, QuickPay fee deduction, insurance deduction, The Haul bonus. Reconciliation accuracy: 99.96%. Failed payment auto-retry: 94% success within 48 hours.

**IVQ-1806: Module 4 — Dispatch Intelligence: Load Board Management** — QPilotOS manages internal and external load boards. Internal: 847 active loads displayed to 2,400 carriers with smart filtering (show only loads matching carrier equipment, endorsement, location). External: auto-post to DAT/Truckstop.com when internal matching insufficient. Load board posting optimization: right price, right time = 34% faster matching.

**IVQ-1807: Module 4 — Dispatch: Multi-Stop Route Optimization** — For dispatchers managing 50+ daily loads, Module 4 optimizes: multi-stop routing (pickup A → delivery B → pickup C → delivery D), driver HOS feasibility, equipment utilization (minimize empty miles), and time-window compliance. Average empty mile reduction: 12% through intelligent multi-stop planning.

**IVQ-1808: Module 4 — Dispatch: Exception Management** — QPilotOS triages 23,000 daily platform events, surfacing only 340 requiring human attention. Exception categories: late pickup (shipper delay), late delivery (traffic/weather), equipment failure, driver emergency, rate dispute, compliance flag. Each exception includes: severity, recommended action, and escalation timer (auto-escalates if unresolved in 30/60/120 minutes).

**IVQ-1809: Module 4 — Dispatch: Capacity Planning** — 7/14/30-day capacity forecast by region. Inputs: committed loads, seasonal patterns, weather forecast, shipper volume commitments, carrier availability. Output: surplus/deficit heat map — guides carrier recruitment and load pricing. Accuracy: 89% at 7-day forecast, 78% at 30-day.

**IVQ-1810: Module 5 — Safety Operations: Real-Time Safety Dashboard** — Live dashboard showing: active driver locations with safety scores, current weather hazards affecting routes, open safety incidents, near-miss reports pending review, dashcam events in coaching queue. Safety Manager can click any driver to see: real-time speed, HOS status, last inspection result, current safety score, The Haul safety badges.

**IVQ-1811: Module 5 — Safety: Incident Command Integration** — When accident occurs, Module 5 activates ICS (Incident Command System) framework: auto-assigns Incident Commander (Safety Manager), creates Incident Action Plan template, tracks resources deployed, manages communication with agencies (FMCSA, NRC, state police), and documents timeline per NIMS requirements.

**IVQ-1812: Module 5 — Safety: Predictive Risk Modeling** — ESANG AI feeds Module 5 with predictive risk scores per load: considering route risk (historical accident data), driver risk (fatigue, experience, safety record), weather risk, cargo risk (hazmat class, quantity), time-of-day risk (circadian), and equipment risk (maintenance status). Composite risk score 0-100 enables: high-risk load intervention (additional monitoring, alternate driver, route change).

**IVQ-1813: Module 5 — Safety: Fleet Benchmarking** — Compares carrier safety performance against: FMCSA industry averages, platform averages, best-in-class benchmarks. Metrics: accident rate, inspection pass rate, BASICs percentiles, near-miss rates, dashcam event rates. Carriers see where they rank and specific improvement recommendations.

**IVQ-1814: Module 6 — Analytics: Custom Report Builder** — Drag-and-drop report builder for all user roles. Pre-built templates: shipper freight analysis, carrier performance scorecard, safety trending, compliance audit, financial summary. Custom reports: any combination of 347 available data fields, filterable by date/region/carrier/shipper/hazmat class. Export: PDF, Excel, PowerPoint, API.

**IVQ-1815: Module 6 — Analytics: Predictive Dashboard** — Forward-looking analytics: revenue forecast (30/60/90 days), demand prediction by lane, carrier churn risk, shipper expansion signals, safety trend projection, compliance risk areas. Uses ESANG AI ML models trained on 2+ years of platform data. Dashboard updates daily with confidence intervals.

**IVQ-1816: Module 6 — Analytics: Data Export & Integration** — API-accessible analytics for enterprise integration: push data to shipper's BI tool (Tableau, PowerBI, Looker), carrier's TMS (TMW, McLeod), or third-party systems. Standard formats: JSON, CSV, XML. Real-time webhooks for event-driven analytics.

**IVQ-1817: Cross-Module Integration: Load Lifecycle** — A single load touches all 6 QPilotOS modules: Module 1 (admin oversight), Module 2 (compliance validation), Module 3 (pricing/invoicing/settlement), Module 4 (dispatch/matching/tracking), Module 5 (safety monitoring), Module 6 (analytics/reporting). Seamless data flow between modules ensures no information silos.

**IVQ-1818: Cross-Module: Emergency Response** — Chemical spill activates all modules simultaneously: Module 5 (safety response), Module 2 (incident reporting), Module 1 (executive notification), Module 3 (insurance claim initiation), Module 4 (load reassignment), Module 6 (incident analytics). Total time from incident to full platform response: <5 minutes.

**IVQ-1819: QPilotOS for Shipper Role** — Shipper's QPilotOS view: load creation wizard, real-time shipment visibility, carrier performance scorecards, cost analytics, compliance documentation access, and invoice management. Simplified interface — shippers see relevant modules only (not dispatcher/safety views).

**IVQ-1820: QPilotOS for Carrier/Catalyst Role** — Carrier's QPilotOS view: available loads, fleet management, driver management, financial dashboard (settlements, aging, revenue), safety scoreboard, compliance status. Carrier sees their performance benchmarked against platform averages.

**IVQ-1821: QPilotOS for Dispatcher Role** — Dispatcher's QPilotOS view: optimized for speed — load board, driver availability, quick dispatch, exception management, HOS countdown clocks. Dispatcher can assign, modify, and track loads with minimal clicks. Average load assignment time: 45 seconds through QPilotOS vs. 8 minutes through traditional workflow.

**IVQ-1822: QPilotOS for Driver (Mobile)** — Driver's mobile QPilotOS: current load details, navigation (hazmat-compliant route), document capture (BOL photo → OCR), DVIR pre-trip checklist, EusoWallet balance, The Haul leaderboard, messaging with dispatch. Works offline with sync when connectivity restored.

**IVQ-1823: QPilotOS for Safety Manager** — Safety Manager's QPilotOS: real-time fleet safety dashboard, incident investigation workspace, dashcam coaching queue, BASICs trending, training management, audit preparation toolkit, and safety culture metrics. All safety data in one place vs. 7+ separate systems in traditional operations.

**IVQ-1824: QPilotOS for Terminal Manager** — Terminal Manager's QPilotOS: appointment schedule, rack utilization dashboard, queue management, vapor recovery monitoring, tank inventory levels, gate access log, emergency response panel, and throughput analytics. Manages entire terminal operation from single screen.
